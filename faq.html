Why?

Have you ever used IXL? Have you always hated how everything has to be in a specific order, otherwise everything is wrong? The main point is IXL is garbage at simple things that other sites can do. And it's still being used by school districts due to contracts (presumably). In fact, in some cases, it's required to use this buggy mess. The goal of this site is simple: To combat it.

Why can't I just use MathPapa?

Recently, they've locked behind some of the features it has (like continued use of the algebra calculator) behind a monthly subscription. This site uses its own version of this, specifically made for each topic.

How do I properly enter text for math equations?

All inputs regarding math use LaTeX. Please refer to the LaTeX reference guide on Wikibooks (Mathematics) for syntax reference.
